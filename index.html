How It Works

A Simple, Transparent Process for Civic Action.

1. See & Snap

See an issue? Open the app, take a photo or video, and the geolocation is automatically tagged.

2. Report & Relax

Select the issue category, add a description, and submit. Your complaint is instantly sent to the correct department.

3. Track & Verify

Track the status of your complaint in real-time. Once it's "Resolved", you'll get an "after" photo to verify the work.

The Accountability Loop

If the work isn't satisfactory, you can **re-open the complaint** with a new photo. This restarts the process and ensures the job gets done right.
