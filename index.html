How It Works

1. See & Snap

See a civic issue? Open the app, take a photo, and tag the location.

2. Report & Relax

Add a description, select a category, and submit. We route it to the right department.

3. Authority Acts

The responsible authority is notified, and an SLA timer starts for resolution.

4. Track & Verify

Track progress in real-time. Verify the fix with an "after" photo. Re-open if not satisfied.
